What is TravelGPT

TravelGPT is an AI chatbot that generates human like responses for any questions you ask related to leisure and travelling. Leveraging our 50-billion parameter large language model we have gathered from proprietary sources, we aim to achieve the following purpose:

  2. To become their personal tour guide when they are at the place they are visiting.
